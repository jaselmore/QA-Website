What is Year Up?

Year Up is a non-profit organization which provides urban youth training in technology, business communication, and professional skills that are needed to navigate their way in Corporate America. After 5 months of training, students will earn an internship at a 500 fortune company.

Year Up currently has 18 locations across the US and is expanding.
Year Up Bay Area has 2 locations: San Francisco & Silicon Valley

Visit Year Up's Website!
