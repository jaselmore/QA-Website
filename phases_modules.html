P h a s e s

L&D
(Learning and Development)

The 1st phase of Year Up is the L&D phase. The L&D phase consist of 3 modules, each 7 weeks long for a total of 21 weeks. Technical, business communications, and professional skills are taught during this time in specific trainings. Grades and an internship readiness assessment (IRA) determine if the student will earn an internship after the 21 weeks.

Internship

After the L&D phase, if earned, the student will go to a 6 month internship at a fortune 500 company.

M o d u l e s

Module I

Module I consist of the 1st 7 weeks. Students will have trainings in Technology Foundations, Web Development Fundamentals, Software Applications (Independent Learning Lab), Business Communications, and Professional Skills. After these 7 weeks, students will an evaluation based on how the student has displayed the ability to be empowered, professional, in-demand, and career ready (EPIC).

Module II

Module II consist of weeks 8-14. Students will have training in Help Desk Solutions and Network/System Administration. Students will also continue their training in Software Applications, Business Communications, and Professional Skills. After these 7 weeks, students will receieve a second and final EPIC evaulation.

Module III

Module III consist of weeks 15-21. Staff places students in specific tracks based on interest and/or skillset. Tracks availble are: IT support, Quality Assurance, Project Coordination, and *Cyber Security. *This track starts during Module I
